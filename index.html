Kochi- The Queen of Arabian Sea

City Information

  • State:Kerala
  • Country:India
  • Native Language:Malayalam
  • Population:3,507,000

Kochi is known as one of the vibrant and busiest city in India.Kochi is the only district in Kerala with its own island.It is bounded by Fort Kochi and Mattanchery in the north, which is one of the main tourist attraction of travellers.Cochin International Airport is World's first fully solar powered airport.Kochi has been culturally identified by the chineese fishing nets which were introduced by the chineese during the 14th century.
